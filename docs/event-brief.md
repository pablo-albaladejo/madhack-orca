## Overview

A 3-hour evening hackathon focused on building AI agents that communicate with each other. The premise: user interfaces are being replaced by conversations with AI agents, and integrations between services are shifting from APIs to AI-to-AI communication.

## Challenge

Teams build two interconnected agents:

- **Consumer agent** — Accepts user requests (e.g., trip planning)
- **Provider agent** — Connected to specific APIs with full CRUD logic, including booking and cancellation

All agents integrate through **Orca** orchestration during final demos.
